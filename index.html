Imposter syndrome

Inposter syndrome is persistant inability to beleive one's success is deserved or has been legitimately achieved as a result of own skill and effort

Five tips to overcome

  1. Talk to someone, you are likely not the only one
  2. Accept positive feedback
  3. keep a log
  4. Embrace positive self-talk
  5. Break out of your comfort zone
